Caesar Salad

Description

A classic Caesar salad with crisp romaine, creamy dressing, crunchy croutons, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large salad bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce and Caesar dressing.
  2. Toss until the lettuce is evenly coated.
  3. Add the croutons and Parmesan cheese and toss again.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately.
