Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that includes layers of pasta, cheese, and meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce and diced tomatoes, and simmer for about 30 minutes.
  4. In a large pot of boiling water, cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 1 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese, and 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Layer with cooked lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese mixture, and meat sauce.
  8. Repeat layers and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Cover with aluminum foil and bake in preheated oven
